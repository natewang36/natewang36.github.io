About Me

Name: Nathan C. Wang
Age: 17
High School: Phillips Academy Andover - Andover, MA
Undergraduate: Haverford College - Haverford, PA

I grew up in Taipei, Taiwan, and was accepted into Phillips Academy Andover in Massachusetts in 2015. At Andover, I developed a passion for producing films and documentaries, and also enjoyed creative writing and computer science. Throughout all this, I was able to continue working with robots on the school's VEX Robotics team, which I became a co-president of in my junior year.

At Haverford College, I am planning to major in interdisciplinary physics and qualify for the 4+1 Engineering Program with the University of Pennsylvania.
